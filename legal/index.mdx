## What belongs here

Use this section for documents such as:

- privacy policy
- terms of service
- data-processing terms
- acceptable use policy
- cookie policy
- notices required for regulated flows

## Publishing rules

- do not publish placeholder language as final legal text
- add an effective date once a document is approved
- keep old versions or change logs where required
- make sure product behavior matches every published statement
