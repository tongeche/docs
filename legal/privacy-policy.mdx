## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how `tibs` collects, uses, and protects
information when people access or use the TIBS platform, including the main
commuter experience, related web applications, and supporting services.

TIBS is a commuter safety information platform that lets users:

- report public transport incidents
- search for vehicle information
- view transport safety signals
- submit ride feedback
- participate in survey and learning activities where available

TIBS is committed to handling information responsibly and in line with
applicable data protection law.

## Scope

This Privacy Policy applies when individuals interact with TIBS through its
websites, applications, and platform services.

This includes situations where a person:

- uses the TIBS website or applications
- submits a transport incident report or observation
- searches for vehicle information or safety insights
- views or interacts with public transport safety data
- participates in feedback, voting, survey, or learning features offered
  through the service

This policy applies only to information collected through the TIBS platform
itself.

## Third-party services and boundaries

TIBS may link to third-party services such as map providers, external resources,
or informational sites.

- this policy does not apply to third-party services
- TIBS does not control those third-party privacy practices
- users should review the privacy policies of those services directly

This policy also does not apply to information collected independently by:

- public transport operators
- SACCO organizations
- transport companies
- government authorities
- other third parties referenced through the platform

## Information we collect

TIBS collects information reasonably necessary to operate and improve the
service.

## Information you provide

Users may voluntarily provide information when using platform features.

### Report data

When someone submits a transport incident report, TIBS may collect:

- a vehicle plate number or public vehicle identifier
- an incident or observation category
- an approximate location or corridor
- an approximate time of the event
- optional notes describing what happened
- optional nearby vehicle details where no exact vehicle was selected

### Optional contact information

Users may optionally provide contact details such as an email address for:

- clarifying a submitted report
- responding to user inquiries
- moderation or follow-up on platform activity

Providing contact details is optional.

### Feedback, voting, and interaction data

Users may also provide information through:

- ride feedback submissions
- incident voting or reaction flows
- optional comments
- evidence uploads such as ride photos
- survey submissions in approved workflows
- learning progress actions in the learning center

## Information collected automatically

When a person accesses TIBS, the platform may collect technical and usage data
automatically, including:

- IP address
- browser type and version
- device type
- operating system
- pages and features used
- approximate location derived from browser or device settings where enabled
- session and security signals needed to operate the service safely

This information helps TIBS:

- run and maintain the platform
- improve performance and reliability
- monitor security and abuse
- understand general usage patterns

## Aggregated and derived data

TIBS may create aggregated or derived statistics from platform activity, such
as:

- incident statistics
- vehicle safety summaries
- corridor safety trends
- route activity insights
- survey performance summaries
- learning completion summaries

Aggregated data is intended not to identify individual users.

## Purpose of processing

TIBS may use collected information to:

- provide the service
- let users submit reports and search vehicles
- show transport safety information and summaries
- operate survey and learning workflows
- detect abuse, spam, or misuse
- improve features and system reliability
- protect the platform and its users from malicious activity
- comply with legal or regulatory obligations

## Legal basis

Where required by applicable law, TIBS processes information on one or more of
the following bases:

- legitimate interests in operating and maintaining a public transport safety
  information platform
- consent, where a user grants permissions such as location access
- legal obligation, where disclosure or retention is required by law

## Data sharing and disclosure

TIBS does not sell personal information.

Limited information may be shared with:

- hosting, infrastructure, storage, and technical service providers
- analytics or operational service providers used to understand performance and
  usage
- legal authorities where disclosure is required by law or legal process
- professional advisers such as lawyers, auditors, or insurers where necessary

## User-generated content

TIBS allows users to submit reports and observations relating to public
transport activity. These submissions are user-generated content.

TIBS operates as a platform for collecting and displaying rider-submitted
observations. It does not independently verify every submission and does not
guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of every user report.

Reports shown on the platform reflect the observations or opinions of the
submitting users and do not necessarily represent the views of TIBS.

### Responsibilities for submissions

Users are responsible for the content they submit. By submitting a report, a
user confirms that the information:

- is based on their own observation or experience
- relates to public transport operations or safety conditions
- is provided in good faith and for informational purposes

Users must not submit content that:

- contains harassment, threats, or abusive language
- includes false, misleading, or intentionally harmful information
- includes unnecessary personal information about identifiable individuals
- targets, shames, or harasses drivers, passengers, or operators

### Moderation

TIBS may:

- review user-submitted reports
- remove or restrict content that violates policy
- reduce visibility of reports considered inaccurate, abusive, or harmful
- restrict users who repeatedly violate platform rules

Moderation may be automated, manual, or community-assisted.

## Data retention

Information is retained only for as long as needed for the purposes described in
this policy.

Typical retention periods may include:

- incident reports retained for analysis and historical reference
- security and system logs retained for operational monitoring
- aggregated statistics retained for long-term product insight

When information is no longer required, it may be deleted or anonymized.

## Your rights

Depending on applicable law, users may have rights to:

- request access to personal data
- request correction of inaccurate data
- request deletion of personal data
- object to certain processing
- request restriction of processing

Requests may be sent through the contact details below.

## Security

TIBS applies reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect
information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

No internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## Children’s privacy

The TIBS platform is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18.

If TIBS becomes aware that personal information from a minor has been collected
without appropriate consent, it will work to remove that information.

## Changes to this policy

TIBS may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect:

- legal requirements
- technology changes
- changes to product features
- changes in operational practices

When updates are made, the effective or last-updated date will be revised.
