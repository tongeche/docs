## What this section covers

The network section brings together the transport structure behind TIBS:

- corridors
- routes
- saccos
- stops and stages

## Why it matters

Network context helps people:

- understand where a report or incident happened
- connect a vehicle to its route or corridor
- explore which areas are currently covered in TIBS
- move more easily through map and search experiences

## What users can expect

Depending on the page, TIBS may show:

- corridor names and summaries
- route relationships
- sacco context
- stop and stage references
- coverage views for supported areas
