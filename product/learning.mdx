## What the learning section offers

The learning experience helps people move through guided material such as:

- courses
- modules
- lessons
- assessments
- progress tracking

## How learning is used

Learning content may be used for:

- onboarding
- readiness guidance
- skills development
- role-specific training

## Progress and completion

Where learning is offered, users may be able to:

- continue from where they left off
- track completed modules
- complete assessments
- work toward readiness or completion milestones

## Access

Some learning content may be public, while other content may be limited to
specific participants or programs.
