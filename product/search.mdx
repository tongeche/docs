## What can be searched

TIBS search helps people find:

- vehicles by plate or public code
- corridors
- stops and stages
- related transport context

## Where search is used

Search supports several common tasks:

- checking a vehicle before boarding
- moving from a vehicle search into reports or feedback
- exploring map locations
- finding the right corridor or stop more quickly

## Why search matters

The platform is designed for quick decision-making. Search reduces the time it
takes to go from uncertainty to a useful vehicle, location, or route context.
