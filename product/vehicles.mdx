## What vehicle pages are for

TIBS vehicle pages help riders look up a vehicle before boarding or review a
vehicle after an experience.

## What a vehicle profile can show

A vehicle profile may include:

- public code or plate reference
- route and corridor context
- operator or sacco context
- recent incident signals
- recent ride feedback
- general safety or service summaries

## How riders use this section

- check a vehicle before boarding
- review recent concerns tied to a vehicle
- understand route and operator context
- share feedback after a trip

## Important note

Vehicle profiles on TIBS are informational. They bring together community and
platform signals, but they are not official operator records.
