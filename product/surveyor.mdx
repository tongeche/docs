## Who this is for

The surveyor section is for approved participants involved in structured field
data collection programs.

It is not part of the general public rider experience.

## What surveyors do

Depending on the project, surveyors may:

- start and end assigned field sessions
- work within specific corridors or assignments
- record vehicle and transport observations
- review their own activity history
- manage expenses or payout details where applicable

## Access and participation

Survey participation is controlled. Access may depend on:

- invitation or approval
- project assignment
- required documents or acceptance steps
- active participation status

## Why this matters to TIBS

Surveyor activity helps strengthen the quality and coverage of transport data
used across TIBS services.
