## How people use TIBS

- report concerns or service issues they observe
- check vehicles before or after a trip
- follow incidents and nearby alerts
- share ride feedback
- explore transport corridors and coverage
- take part in survey or learning programs where available

## Restricted areas

Some parts of TIBS are for approved surveyors, administrators, or project
participants only. Public and restricted experiences are kept separate.
