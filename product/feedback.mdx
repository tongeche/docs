## What feedback is for

Ride feedback helps TIBS understand how transport experiences feel from the
passenger point of view.

It supports:

- service-quality visibility
- vehicle-level summaries
- recurring issue detection
- better rider awareness over time

## What users can submit

Feedback may include:

- an overall ride sentiment
- selected issue or experience tags
- an optional written comment
- an optional photo where supported

## How feedback appears

Individual feedback contributes to broader summaries such as:

- overall sentiment
- common themes
- recent rider concerns

These summaries help people quickly understand the kind of experience other
users are reporting.

## Good feedback rules

- describe the actual ride experience
- keep comments relevant and respectful
- avoid sharing unnecessary personal information
- only upload images that relate directly to the ride or issue being reported
