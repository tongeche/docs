## What incident views show

TIBS incident views help people understand current or recent safety-related
events across the transport network.

Incident information may appear through:

- map views
- nearby alerts
- incident detail pages
- vehicle and corridor context

## What users can expect

Incident pages are designed to show:

- what happened
- where it happened
- when it was observed
- the transport context around the event
- community signals where available

## How to read incident information

- incident information may be based on rider or community reporting
- new information can change how an incident is shown
- some incidents may remain visible only for a limited time
- the platform is meant to improve awareness, not to replace official channels

## Community input

Where available, users may help confirm or challenge incident visibility through
community feedback. This helps TIBS distinguish stronger signals from weaker
ones over time.
