## What it is

TIBS is the main public service in the platform. It is where riders and general
users interact with the core transport-safety experience.

## What people use it for

People use TIBS to:

- report safety or service concerns
- check a vehicle before or after a ride
- follow incidents and nearby alerts
- share ride feedback
- explore corridors, routes, and transport context

## Main experiences

The main TIBS service includes:

- reporting
- incidents and alerts
- vehicle lookup
- ride feedback
- map and corridor exploration
- account and support pages

## Who it is for

This service is primarily for:

- riders
- commuters
- public users
- partners who need the main TIBS experience

## Service address

`https://tibs.tisabs.com`

## How it connects to the rest of TIBS

The main TIBS service connects with the wider platform by:

- sharing sign-in with other connected TIBS services
- linking into survey and learning journeys where relevant
- using the same transport context across the platform
