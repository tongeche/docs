## What it is

The learning center is the TIBS service for guided learning, readiness support,
and course-based progress.

It is separate from the main rider experience, but it remains connected to the
wider TIBS platform.

## What it is used for

The learning center can be used for:

- onboarding
- guided learning
- assessments
- role-specific training
- progress tracking

## Main experiences

Users may find experiences such as:

- courses
- modules and lessons
- assessments
- account and progress pages
- privacy and terms information related to the learning service

## Who it is for

Depending on the program, the learning center may serve:

- public users
- survey participants
- program-specific learners
- teams using TIBS readiness or onboarding material

## Service address

`https://tibs-learning-center.netlify.app`

## How it connects to the rest of TIBS

The learning center connects with the wider platform by:

- sharing sign-in with connected TIBS services
- supporting readiness and onboarding journeys
- linking learning progress to specific programs where applicable
