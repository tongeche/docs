## What it is

The survey and operations service supports approved programs that rely on
structured fieldwork, assignments, and operational review.

It is not part of the general public rider experience.

## What it is used for

This service supports work such as:

- survey participation
- field assignments
- vehicle and route observations
- project-based operational workflows
- expense and payout-related processes

## Main experiences

Participants may use this service to:

- sign in to approved programs
- start and end field sessions
- claim or release assignments
- submit survey observations
- manage approved workflow steps related to participation

Administrators may also use it to:

- review submissions
- manage assignments
- support participant operations

## Who it is for

This service is for:

- approved surveyors
- project participants
- program coordinators
- authorized administrators

## Service address

`https://vdc.tisabs.com`

## How it connects to the rest of TIBS

The survey service connects with the wider platform by:

- supporting structured data collection
- sharing sign-in with other TIBS services
- linking participation requirements, legal documents, and operations workflows
