## How TIBS fits together

TIBS has three connected experiences:

- the rider experience for public reporting, incidents, vehicles, feedback,
  search, and network information
- the survey experience for structured fieldwork and participant workflows
  when access is granted
- the learning experience for onboarding, progress tracking, and readiness
  where learning is offered

These experiences are linked, but some areas are public and others are
restricted.

## A simple way to use TIBS

1. Open the page that matches your task.
2. Read the page details before submitting anything.
3. Use only the information you observed or experienced.
4. Check the policy pages if you are posting content.
5. Use survey or learning pages only if you have been invited or approved.
